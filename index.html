Life is not the same

9-21-23

Time doesn’t feel the same with out you. Before it went by too quickly yet it felt to stand still. I savored every moment I could with you. Every touch, every kiss, every hello felt like nourishment to my soul. Now I will never experience those beautiful moments again. June 3rd 2023 was the last day I spent with you before you passed away. It still doesn’t feel real. I constantly hope that I’ll wake from a coma and see you sitting next to my bed waiting for me to wake up. I just want to talk to you again. To feel you again. To have a future in our hearts again. August 18th 2023 would have been the day we got married. I was going to surprise you with a trip to the church and get it done between you me and our friends. Then later we could have our big wedding with your whole family in the Philippines. It would have been a beautiful day. I know you would have cried the whole time but after it was over we would be able to call each other Mr and Mrs and mean it! We got so used to calling each other husband and wife and that point it really wouldn’t have changed much other than making it official. I loved you so much my little Jellybean. I still love you like you never left. I don’t think I can love another like I did you. You were my soul mate. I had never felt so at peace and so safe. I had never felt so ready to put my life on the line for someone before. I never wanted a family before you. I was ready and I couldn’t wait to meet our kids…now we never will. Its so hard to write these words because I know they are true.
I am making this blog to help get my thoughts together. As of now everything is so fragmented. I am also trying to pull my mind out of the gutter of grief and do good in the name of my beloved. She was the kindest most gentle and caring human I had ever met. I want to try and continue with her example. While I don’t think I will ever have children without her, I can at least try to spread goodness how ever I can. I will be making this website all on my own and use it to learn many new things and hopefully in time this might be a place for others like me to come and find some peace or connection to the chaos of existence.
I love you Jellybean
-B
